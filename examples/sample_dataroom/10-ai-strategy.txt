AI Strategy and Roadmap

Stated AI strategy: Management has published an AI transformation roadmap
in the 2025 annual report. Three-pillar plan: (1) demand forecasting
enhancement, (2) sales rep productivity tooling, (3) customer self-service
ordering. No timeline commitments on pillar (3).

Deployed AI: Demand forecasting model described in the product section is
the only production AI system. No generative AI, no LLMs, no foundation
models currently in use. No AI-native client onboarding or agent-facing
APIs exposed.

RAG / retrieval augmented systems: None in production.

MLOps maturity: Basic. Model retraining is a manual quarterly process. No
experiment tracking, no model registry, no automated deployment pipeline.
Ownership sits entirely within the 6-person data team.

AI governance policy: Drafted Q4 2025, not yet ratified. Covers employee
use of public LLM tools. Does not address enterprise AI procurement or
model-risk management.

AI-native positioning: The company does not describe itself or its products
as AI-native or AI-first. Leadership acknowledges gap versus US competitors
(Sysco Intelligent Kitchen, GFS GenAI menu engineering) but has not made
material capital commitments to close it.
