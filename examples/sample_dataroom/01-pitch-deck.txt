Colabor Group — Executive Summary

Company overview: Colabor Group is a Quebec-headquartered foodservice distribution
company founded in 1964. Our mission is to supply independent restaurants and
institutional kitchens with a broad assortment of fresh and specialty products.

Who we are: We operate as the largest independent foodservice distributor in
Eastern Canada. Our vision is to be the trusted partner for every local operator.

Sector positioning: We sit between national broadline distributors (Sysco, GFS)
and regional specialty houses. The industry we serve — foodservice distribution —
is a $95B Canadian category growing ~2% annually.

Business description: We purchase from ~2,000 upstream producers and sell into
~12,000 restaurant and institutional accounts across Ontario, Quebec, and the
Maritimes.
