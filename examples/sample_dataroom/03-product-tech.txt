Product and Technology Platform

Our core product is a foodservice distribution platform combining catalog,
ordering, delivery routing, and receivables functions. We operate on a
hybrid tech stack: SAP S/4HANA ERP, custom microservices for order
intake and route optimization, and a PostgreSQL database layer.

Architecture: Three regional fulfillment hubs (Montreal, Toronto, Halifax)
connected to a central order-management platform. We expose limited B2B
integration via EDI and a partner API.

Feature set for customers: catalog search, inventory availability, standing
orders, delivery tracking, and invoicing. No self-service onboarding; our
sales representatives provision every account.

Engineering team: 28 FTEs split between platform (14), data (6), and
infrastructure (8). We run on AWS with Datadog observability.

Machine learning: demand forecasting model in production (gradient-boosted
trees on ~3 years of SKU-level order history). No generative AI deployed.
