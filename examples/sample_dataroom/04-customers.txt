Customer and Contract Overview

Customer base: ~12,000 active accounts as of Q1 2026. 78% are independent
restaurants, 14% are institutional (schools, healthcare), 8% are regional
chain operators.

Logo concentration: Top 10 customers represent 9.2% of revenue. Top 50
customers represent 22% of revenue. No single customer exceeds 1.5% of
revenue — customer concentration risk is low.

Retention: Dollar-based net retention of 94% over trailing twelve months.
Gross logo churn of 11.3% — elevated versus 8.4% in FY2023, driven by
independent-restaurant mortality.

Contracts: Most relationships are governed by standard Master Service
Agreements with 30-day termination. Approximately 340 accounts are on
multi-year pricing contracts. No enterprise MSAs over $1M annual value.

Case studies: Three published testimonials from flagship Quebec
restaurant groups highlighting on-time delivery metrics above 98%.

NPS: Latest customer survey NPS score of 42, up from 38 in FY2023.
