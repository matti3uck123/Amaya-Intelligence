Operations and Supply Chain

Fulfillment infrastructure: Three distribution warehouses covering Montreal
(420,000 sq ft), Toronto (280,000 sq ft), and Halifax (95,000 sq ft). Each
houses dry, refrigerated, and frozen inventory.

Supplier network: ~2,100 upstream producers. Top 50 suppliers represent 38%
of procurement spend. Supplier contracts negotiated annually; no long-term
exclusivity arrangements.

Procurement: Purchase order generation is semi-automated via our ERP. Demand
forecasting feeds a weekly replenishment cycle. Vendor-managed inventory
pilots underway with three produce suppliers.

Delivery fleet: 420 refrigerated trucks, split roughly 70% owned / 30%
contracted. Route optimization runs nightly on in-house software; dynamic
re-routing for same-day orders introduced in 2024.

Last-mile delivery: All routes serviced by in-house drivers. Average drops
per route: 14. On-time delivery rate: 98.2% over trailing twelve months.

Logistics systems: ERP is SAP S/4HANA. Warehouse management is SAP EWM.
Transportation management is custom-built on top of Descartes.
