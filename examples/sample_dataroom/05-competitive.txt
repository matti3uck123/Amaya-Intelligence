Competitive Landscape

Primary competitors in the Canadian foodservice distribution category:

Sysco Canada — national broadline, ~$5B Canadian revenue. Investing heavily
in AI-driven customer platforms (Sysco Intelligent Kitchen initiative) and
digital self-service. Our principal competitor in the independent-restaurant
segment. Sysco has a commanding market share on chain accounts.

Gordon Food Service (GFS) — also national, similar scale. Positioning
overlaps on institutional and healthcare. GFS has announced a partnership
with a generative-AI vendor for menu engineering — a differentiation
challenge.

Specialty regional distributors — dozens of smaller competitors focused on
single product categories (seafood, bakery, Italian imports). Each captures
2-5% share in their niche.

Market positioning: We compete primarily on local service depth and
Quebec-market specialization versus the national incumbents. Our
differentiation is rep relationships and regional product assortment —
moat characteristics that AI-mediated ordering challenges directly.

Versus the Gartner Supply Chain Top 25, we lag on every technology metric.
