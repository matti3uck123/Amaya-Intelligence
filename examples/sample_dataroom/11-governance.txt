Board and Governance Summary

Board of directors: Eight members. Five independent directors, two insider
directors (CEO and founding family representative), one chairman (independent).

Chairman: Jean-Paul Gauthier, independent, appointed 2022. 30-year
foodservice industry veteran; former CEO of a national broadline
competitor.

Audit committee: Three independent directors. Charter last updated 2024.
Meets quarterly. Chair: Dr. Sylvie Pelletier (CPA, former Deloitte partner).

Compensation committee: Three independent directors. Approves executive
compensation, equity grants, and incentive plan design.

Nominating and governance committee: Four independent directors. Reviews
board composition and succession planning annually.

Bylaws: Last amended 2021. Standard TSX-listed corporation structure.
Proxy access provisions added 2023.

Shareholder base: Institutional ownership 42% (top holders include Fidelity
Canada, CPPIB, Caisse de dépôt). Retail float 50%. Insider and founding-family
holdings 8%.

Governance policies: Code of conduct, whistleblower policy, insider trading
policy, related-party transaction policy. All reviewed annually.
